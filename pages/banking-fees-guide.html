FAQ

Common questions

Are overdraft fees still common?

Yes, although many banks have reduced or removed them, overdraft practices still vary widely and should be reviewed before opening an account.

What is the cheapest checking account setup?

A practical low-cost setup often combines a no-monthly-fee checking account, in-network ATM access, and overdraft controls turned on.

Do credit unions usually charge fewer fees?

Many credit unions offer competitive fee structures, but the best choice still depends on branch access, ATM reach, and account features.
