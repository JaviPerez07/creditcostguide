A practical example helps. FAQ

Common questions

What is the minimum credit score for a mortgage?

It depends on the loan type. FHA, conventional, VA, and USDA programs all work differently, and lender overlays can raise the practical minimum.

Can I buy a home with a 580 score?

Possibly through FHA, especially if you qualify for the program and have the necessary down payment and documentation.

Why do mortgage lenders use different score standards than card issuers?

Mortgage loans are larger, longer, and more heavily documented, so underwriting is more conservative and model-specific.

Does a bigger down payment help if my score is lower?

Yes. A larger down payment can reduce lender risk and improve the overall file, though it does not erase every score issue.

Should I improve my score before shopping?

If time allows, even moderate improvement can widen lender choice and lower the monthly payment over a long loan term.
