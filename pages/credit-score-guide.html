FAQ

Common questions

How fast can a credit score improve?

Some changes like lower reported balances can help quickly, while rebuilding payment history and account age usually takes longer.

Do checking accounts affect credit scores?

Standard checking and savings accounts typically do not report to the major credit bureaus unless an account goes unpaid and is sent to collections.

Can paying off a loan hurt my score?

A score can dip temporarily because the account closes and your mix changes, but lower debt and stronger finances usually matter more over time.
